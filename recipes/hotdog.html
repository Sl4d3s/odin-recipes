hotdog

This 'burger dog' is far more than a hot dog-shaped cheeseburger. It's a one-handed wonder that's perfectly proportioned and easier to eat than a regular cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef
  • chedar chese
  • hot dog buns
  • ketchup
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place half the ground beef on a sheet of plastic wrap.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate
  3. Toast hot dog buns on the grill and spread each with 2 teaspoons mayonnaise
